Why Youhu Why Youhu

很多人并不缺建议。
他们缺的是一个足够安静的地方，去看见自己。 Most people are not short on advice.
They are short on spaces quiet enough to see themselves.

Youhu 想提供的，不是更强的管理感，而是更深的自我靠近。它不催你更快变好，只想陪你更真实地靠近“我是谁”。

Youhu is not here to optimize you into emotional efficiency. It is here to help you move closer to yourself, and to the deeper question beneath mood: who am I, really?

“我们想做的，不是一个让人更会管理情绪的产品，而是一片让人重新靠近自己的海。”

“We are not building a product that trains people to manage feelings better. We are building a sea that helps people come back to themselves.”
