Application Application

回答 20 道创世问题，看看你是否真的属于最早的这一百位。 Answer twenty founder questions and see whether you truly belong in the earliest hundred.

这不是一张泛泛的报名表。我们会用 20 道问题看你是否真的有长期自我记录习惯、是否愿意回看自己、是否能容纳复杂情绪，以及你会不会成为有价值的 Founder 共建者。

This is not a generic signup form. These 20 questions help us see whether you already have a long-term reflective habit, whether you revisit yourself over time, whether you can hold emotional complexity, and whether you would become a valuable founder collaborator.

第一部分 / 5 Part 1 / 5

先确认你已经有一套和自己相处的方式。 First, we want to understand whether you already have a real practice of working with yourself.

这四题看的是你有没有稳定习惯，而不是一时兴趣。

These four questions are about habit depth, not momentary curiosity.

1. 过去 30 天，你实际用过哪些方式整理自己？ 1. In the last 30 days, which practices did you actually use to process yourself?

可多选。

Choose all that apply.

2. 最近一个月，你平均每周会主动记录几次？ 2. Over the last month, how many times per week did you intentionally record something about yourself?

3. 你现在最常依赖哪一种方式？ 3. Which option do you currently rely on the most?

4. 你和“记录自己”这件事，大概持续多久了？ 4. How long have you had any ongoing practice of recording yourself?

第二部分 / 5 Part 2 / 5

接下来，我们看你如何和情绪相处。 Next, we look at how you actually move with emotion.

这四题会暴露你的真实模式，而不是你理想中的自己。

These four questions are meant to surface your actual patterns, not your ideal self-image.

5. 你最希望一个产品真正接住你的时刻，通常是什么时刻？ 5. When do you most want a product to genuinely hold you?

6. 当你状态不好时，你的第一反应通常是什么？ 6. When you are not doing well, what is your usual first response?

7. 下面哪种描述最像你？ 7. Which description feels closest to you?

8. 你会回看自己以前的记录吗？ 8. Do you ever revisit your previous records?

第三部分 / 5 Part 3 / 5

然后，我们看你的反思深度。 Then we look at the depth of your reflective practice.

这四题主要看你是不是只想宣泄，还是已经在追踪自己的长期轮廓。

These four questions help us tell whether you mostly want release, or whether you are already tracking a longer shape of yourself.

9. 你记录时，最想得到的是什么？ 9. When you record yourself, what do you most hope to get from it?

10. 对你来说，最难长期坚持的是哪一件事？ 10. Which part is hardest for you to maintain over time?

11. 你愿意把还没整理好的情绪原样写下来吗？ 11. Are you willing to write down emotions before they are cleaned up or organized?

12. 如果你能长期看见一种变化，你最想看见哪一类？ 12. If you could track one kind of change over time, which would matter most to you?

第四部分 / 5 Part 4 / 5

最后确认你和 Youhu 的匹配度。 Now we check how you fit with Youhu itself.

这四题看的是气质、关系和 Founder 共建能力。

These four questions are about resonance, relationship, and founder-level collaboration.

13. 你第一次被 Youhu 的哪一部分吸引？ 13. Which part of Youhu pulled you in first?

14. 如果 Youhu 对你真的有价值，它更像什么？ 14. If Youhu becomes genuinely valuable to you, what would it feel like?

15. 如果进入创世计划，你愿意连续 3 周给出真实反馈吗？ 15. If accepted, are you willing to give honest feedback for three continuous weeks?

16. 你更适合哪种反馈方式？ 16. Which feedback style fits you best?

第五部分 / 5 Part 5 / 5

最后四题，留给真正重要的部分。 The last four questions are where the real signal usually appears.

请尽量具体。泛泛而谈很难帮助我们判断你是否真的适合成为 Founder。

Please be concrete. General answers make it hard for us to understand whether you are truly a founder fit.

17. 最近一次你强烈觉得“我需要被理解，但我说不清”的时刻是什么？ 17. Tell us about the most recent moment when you strongly felt “I need to be understood, but I cannot explain myself.”

18. 你身上有没有一个反复出现、但一直没真正看清的模式？ 18. Is there a recurring pattern in you that keeps returning, yet still feels unresolved or unseen?

19. 如果 6 个月后 Youhu 对你真的很重要，它帮助你改变了什么？ 19. If Youhu becomes genuinely important to you six months from now, what would it have changed?

20. 你会成为怎样的一位 Founder？你会带来什么样的使用深度或反馈价值？ 20. What kind of founder would you be, and what kind of usage depth or feedback value would you bring?

筛选重点 Selection focus

  • 已有长期习惯 Already has a real practice 我们优先选择已经在长期记录、回看、整理自己的人，而不是一时兴起的人。 We prioritize people who already record, revisit, and reflect over time rather than those arriving on impulse.
  • 能容纳复杂情绪 Can hold emotional complexity 我们在找的不是“情绪管理”用户，而是愿意面对混乱、也愿意留下痕迹的人。 We are not looking for simple mood-management users. We are looking for people willing to face complexity and leave traces behind.
  • 愿意长期共建 Willing to co-build over time 我们优先选择愿意持续使用、真实反馈、并把 Founder 身份当作长期关系的人。 We prioritize people who will keep using the product, give honest feedback, and treat founder status as a long-term relationship.
