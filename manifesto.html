What We Refuse What We Refuse

我们不是在制造另一套“情绪管理方法”。 We are not building another “emotion management method.”

很多时候，人以为自己需要的是方法、出口、建议。但更深处，人真正需要的，常常是一次不被打断的自我看见。

Very often, people think they need methods, relief, or advice. Deeper down, what they really need is an uninterrupted act of seeing themselves.

  • 不是控制感 Not about control

    我们不想把自我觉察做成又一套任务管理，而是想让人真实地靠近自己。

    We are not turning self-awareness into another productivity stack. We want people to move honestly toward themselves.

  • 不是即时止痛 Not instant relief

    Youhu 的价值不是让你立刻感觉更好，而是让你长期更看见自己。

    The value is not to make you feel better instantly, but to help you see yourself more clearly over time.

  • 不是流量关系 Not a traffic game

    最重要的用户不是最多的人，而是最深的人。

    The most important users are not the most numerous. They are the deepest.

Four Principles Four Principles

Youhu 的四条底层原则 The four principles under Youhu

01

觉察比控制更重要 Awareness before control

先看见，才能谈改变。没有被看见的感受，只会继续在暗处支配你。

You have to see before you can change. Feelings that remain unseen continue to govern from the dark.

02

长期痕迹比短期刺激更重要 Traces over stimulation

真正有价值的不是瞬间高潮，而是你几年后仍然能回望的精神痕迹。

What matters is not a spike of instant feeling, but a trace you can revisit years later.

03

允许混乱先存在 Let confusion arrive first

你不需要先说得漂亮、整理正确，才配进入这片海。

You do not need to be articulate or orderly before you deserve entry into this sea.

04

理解可以慢一点发生 Understanding can arrive slowly

Youhu 不催你更快变好，也不逼你更快解释清楚。它给理解留出时间。

Youhu does not rush you toward improvement or immediate coherence. It leaves room for understanding to take time.

如果你也在认真寻找“我是谁”，就从创世申请开始。 If you are seriously trying to answer who you are, start with the founder application.

创世计划不是开放注册，而是一轮对深度用户的筛选。我们更愿意与你一起慢慢做出一款真正长期有效的产品。

The founder program is not open signup. It is a deliberate selection of people willing to go deep and help shape a product built for long-term truth.
