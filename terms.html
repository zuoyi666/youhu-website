Youhu 是一款用于日常回望的产品，不是医疗服务。 Youhu is a product for everyday reflection, not a medical service.

Youhu 提供记录、回响、星图与时光琥珀等体验，帮助用户更看见自己的感受与模式。这些内容不构成医疗诊断、临床建议或危机替代方案。

Youhu offers recording, reflective replies, a constellation view, and time-based memory surfaces to help users see their own feelings and patterns more clearly. None of this is a medical diagnosis, clinical treatment, or a substitute for emergency care.

1. 产品定位 1. Product scope

Youhu 是一款用于日常情绪记录、反思与非评判式倾听的独立产品。它不构成医疗服务，也不是现实世界危机处置机构。

Youhu is an independent product for everyday emotional reflection, journaling, and non-judgmental listening. It is not a medical service and it is not an emergency intervention provider.

2. 创世身份与权益 2. Founder status and entitlements

Founder 编号仅授予最早完成激活的 100 位用户。前 100 位创世领航员可终身免费使用 Youhu 面向个人用户的标准付费功能与会员内容。

Founder numbers are reserved only for the earliest 100 approved users who complete activation. The first 100 founders receive lifetime access to Youhu's standard paid features and membership content for individual users.

该权益不默认覆盖未来可能出现的企业版、线下项目、第三方代收项目或特别注明独立定价的合作内容。创世身份、邀请码与相关规则仍可能随着测试阶段、资格规则和产品结构演进而调整。

This entitlement does not automatically include future enterprise offerings, offline programs, third-party pass-through charges, or collaborations explicitly marked with separate pricing. Founder status, invite mechanics, and related rules may still evolve with testing stages, qualification rules, and product structure changes.

3. 订阅与可用性 3. Subscriptions and availability

Premium 订阅会按你选择的周期自动续期，除非你在当前订阅期结束前取消。价格、账单币种和续费规则以后续正式上架页面为准。某些功能也可能随着测试阶段、地区、语言和账号权益而变化。

Premium subscriptions renew automatically on the cadence you choose unless canceled before the end of the current billing period. Pricing, billing currency, and renewal rules follow the official listing once public distribution opens. Feature availability may also vary by testing stage, region, language, and account entitlements.

4. 现实世界边界 4. Real-world boundary

如果你正在经历急性自伤风险、他伤风险，或现实世界中的紧急危险，请立刻联系你所在地区的危机援助资源或本地紧急服务，而不是仅依赖应用内回信。

If you are experiencing acute risk of self-harm, harm to others, or any real-world emergency, contact your local crisis resources or emergency services immediately rather than relying on replies inside the app.
