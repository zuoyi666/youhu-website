Privacy Policy for Youhu (存在之海)

Last updated: March 14, 2026

1. Data Storage (本地存储声明)

Youhu is designed with your absolute privacy in mind. All your psychological explorations, subconscious texts, and star map data are stored locally on your device. We do not upload, read, or share your personal entries to any external servers.
(《Youhu》将您的绝对隐私放在首位。您所有的心理探索、潜意识文本和星图数据均仅存储于您的本地设备。我们绝不会将您的个人记录上传、读取或分享至任何外部服务器。)

2. Analytics and Payments (分析与支付数据)

To process subscriptions, we use RevenueCat (a third-party payment infrastructure). RevenueCat only receives anonymous purchase IDs to verify your "Pro" status. We do not have access to your credit card information or Apple ID details.
(为了处理订阅，我们使用了 RevenueCat 作为第三方支付基础设施。它仅接收匿名的购买凭证以验证您的 Pro 状态。我们无法获取您的信用卡信息或 Apple ID 详情。)
