这不是三个独立功能，而是一条连续的内在路径。

These are not three isolated features, but one continuous inner path.

01

存在之海 Sea of Being

先让那团还没整理好的东西有地方落下，而不是要求你先解释正确。

First, give the unfinished part of you a place to land instead of demanding immediate coherence.

它接住什么 What it holds

当你说不清、理不顺、甚至只剩碎片时，也可以先把它放进来。

When you cannot explain yourself and only fragments remain, it still gives that state a place to arrive.

它不要求什么 What it does not require

它不要求你先像一个“正常人”那样表达，也不要求你先整理清楚。

It does not require you to sound polished or to organize yourself before entering.

它真正给你的 What it gives

一种低压、非评判、可以长期被回到的入口。

A low-pressure, non-judgmental entrance you can return to over time.

02

灵魂星图 Soul Constellation

把反复出现的感受、关键词和特质，慢慢连成一张只属于你的图。

Slowly connect repeating feelings, keywords, and traits into a map that belongs only to you.

它看见什么 What it notices

每一次留下的关键词、反复出现的感受和渐渐浮出的特质，都会在时间里连接成图。

Keywords, recurring feelings, and slowly surfacing traits begin to connect over time.

它不是什么 What it is not

灵魂星图不是为了量化你，也不是为了给你打分。

The constellation is not there to quantify you or grade your inner life.

它真正留下什么 What it leaves behind

那些原本会被生活噪音吞没的长期线索，终于开始有了形状。

Long-range signals that daily noise would erase finally begin to take shape.

03

时光琥珀 Time Amber

不是所有时刻都该被清空。有些东西，值得被时间轻轻包裹。

Not every moment should be cleared away. Some things deserve to be held gently by time.

它保留什么 What it preserves

那些你以为已经过去、却还会在未来重新发光的瞬间。

Moments you think are over, but that may light up again in the future.

它允许什么 What it allows

让你在未来某一天重新回看、重新理解、重新命名。

It lets you return later to reinterpret, rename, and understand with new eyes.

它为什么重要 Why it matters

有些理解不是当下就能完成，它需要时间成为你的同盟。

Some understanding cannot happen immediately. It needs time to become your ally.

Why it matters Why it matters

Youhu 不是一个让你“更会管理情绪”的工具。 Youhu is not here to make you “better at managing emotions.”

它更像一套低压、长期、非评判的自我认识结构。记录情绪只是入口，真正重要的是你最终能否在这些痕迹中重新认出自己。

It is a low-pressure, long-range, non-judgmental structure for self-recognition. Mood logging is only the entrance. What matters is whether you can eventually recognize yourself inside the traces you keep.

  • 低压 Low pressure

    不是一套督促你持续优化自己的机制，而是一种让你慢下来回望自己的结构。

    Not a mechanism that keeps pushing you to optimize yourself, but a structure that lets you slow down and return.

  • 长期 Long-range

    不是解决一晚上的情绪，而是陪你走过一段可以被重新理解的时间。

    Not something that solves one difficult night, but a path that stays with you long enough to be reinterpreted.

  • 非评判 Non-judgmental

    它不要求你先成为更好的人，才值得被记录和被理解。

    It does not require you to become better first before you deserve to be recorded and understood.
