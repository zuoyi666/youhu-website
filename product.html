三个入口，通向同一个问题：You Who Three entry points, one shared question: You Who

Youhu 的产品结构不是功能堆叠，而是一条连续的内在旅程：先允许存在，再看见模式，最后让时间重新解释那些你以为已经过去的东西。

Youhu is not a stack of disconnected features. It is a continuous inner journey: first allow what is here, then notice the pattern, then let time reinterpret what you thought had already passed.

Journey sequence Journey sequence
存在之海 → 灵魂星图 → 时光琥珀 Sea of Being → Soul Constellation → Time Amber
01 01

存在之海 Sea of Being

当你说不清、理不顺、甚至只剩碎片时，也可以先把它放进来。存在之海的价值，不是让你说得更像一个“正常人”，而是让你在混乱里也有一个可以被接住的地方。

When you cannot explain yourself and only fragments remain, the Sea of Being still lets it arrive. Its value is not in making you sound coherent, but in making sure even the unfinished part of you has somewhere to land.

02 02

灵魂星图 Soul Constellation

每一次留下的关键词、反复出现的感受和渐渐浮出的特质，都会在时间里连接成图。灵魂星图不是为了量化你，而是帮助你看见那些原本会被生活噪音吞没的长期线索。

Keywords, repeating feelings, and the traits that keep surfacing begin to connect over time. The constellation is not there to quantify you, but to reveal long-range signals that everyday noise would otherwise erase.

03 03

时光琥珀 Time Amber

不是所有东西都该被立刻删除或“清空”。有些时刻，值得被时间轻轻包裹，让你在未来的某一天重新回看、重新理解、重新命名。

Not everything should be deleted or cleared immediately. Some moments deserve to be wrapped in time so that you can come back later and reinterpret them with new eyes.

Why it matters Why it matters

Youhu 不是一个让你“更会管理情绪”的工具。 Youhu is not here to make you “better at managing emotions.”

它更像一套低压、长期、非评判的自我认识结构。记录情绪只是入口，真正重要的是你最终能否在这些痕迹中重新认出自己。

It is a low-pressure, long-range, non-judgmental structure for self-recognition. Mood logging is only the entrance. What matters is whether you can eventually recognize yourself inside the traces you keep.
